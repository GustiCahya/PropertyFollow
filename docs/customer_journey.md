# Customer Journey Guide (customer_journey.md)

## Overview

Panduan ini menjelaskan alur lengkap perjalanan klien dari pertama kali mengenal layanan hingga menjadi klien jangka panjang.

Tujuan:

* Menstandarkan pengalaman klien
* Meningkatkan conversion rate
* Memastikan delivery konsisten

---

## 1. Awareness Stage

### Source:

* Referral
* Outreach
* Website

### Goal:

* Menarik perhatian
* Menunjukkan problem yang relevan

### Key Message:

* Banyak booking hilang karena slow response & no follow-up

---

## 2. Interest Stage

### Trigger:

* Klik website
* DM / WhatsApp

### Action:

* Kirim:

  * Penjelasan singkat
  * Use case

### Goal:

* Membuat klien relate dengan problem

---

## 3. Consultation Stage

### Format:

* Call / meeting

### Goal:

* Menggali kondisi bisnis
* Mengidentifikasi loss

---

## 4. Evaluation Stage

### Action:

* Hitung:

  * Revenue loss
  * Potential gain

### Output:

* Insight ke klien

---

## 5. Offer Stage

### Action:

* Present solusi
* Mapping ke package

### Goal:

* Klien memahami value

---

## 6. Closing Stage

### Action:

* Present pricing
* Handle objection

### Goal:

* Deal signed

---

## 7. Onboarding Stage

### Action:

* Kirim onboarding form
* Setup sistem

---

## 8. Delivery Stage

### Action:

* Build automation
* Testing
* Go-live

---

## 9. Retention Stage

### Action:

* Monitoring
* Optimization
* Upsell

---

## 10. Expansion Stage

### Action:

* Upsell ke:

  * Guest journey
  * Reporting

---

## Conclusion

Journey ini memastikan setiap klien melewati proses yang terstruktur dari awareness hingga expansion.
